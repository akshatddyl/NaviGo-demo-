NaviGo

Your guide in the great indoors.

Empowering Independence

NaviGo is a hackathon prototype designed to help visually impaired individuals navigate complex indoor spaces. Using your phone's camera, it allows you to record and replay paths with clear, turn-by-turn audio guidance.

This is a hackathon prototype. It is not a complete product and is intended for demonstration purposes only.

Get the App

Download the Android `.apk` file directly. You will need to enable "Install from unknown sources" on your Android device to install it.
